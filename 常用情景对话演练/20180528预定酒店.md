2018-5-28预定酒店
===
## 原文
****
A: Good afternoon! Welcome to Haixue Hotel. May I help you?<br>

B: Yes. I'd like to check in, please.<br>

A: Yes, sir. May I have your name, please ? And have you made a reservation ? <br>

B: I'm Jimmy Zhang. I have a reservation for a double room for two nights.<br>

A: Yes, I have the record right here.  Could you fill out this registration form, please? And may I see your passport? <br>

B: Sure, here you are.<br>

A: Thank you. Here is your keycard. Your room number is 365 on the third floor.<br>


A: 下午好，欢迎光临海学酒店，有什么可以帮到您吗？<br>

B: 是的，我想办理入住手续。<br>

A: 好的，先生。怎么称呼您？您有预定房间了吗？<br>

B: 我叫张吉米。我预定了一间双人房，住两个晚上。<br>

A: 是的，我这儿有您的记录。您可以填下这份登记表吗？我可以看看您的护照吗？<br>

B: 当然，给你。<br>

A: 谢谢，这是您的房卡。您的房间在3楼，房间号是365。<br>
